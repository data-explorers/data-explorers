Description
I found this in some soil I bought, and put in a bin outside. The bin was covered so the soil was in the dark for some weeks. Only a small portion of the soil was damp. The rest was dry. I noticed that in some patches, there were yellow many things all over the soil. The tiny yellow balls appear to be connected by some light thin filaments. Many were attached to pieces of wood/wood chips in the soil.
