Description
With the surf at 10-12 ft., many jellies and fragments were washed ashore. The first 3 photos were taken at 11 AM. with the high tide at 5.6 ft.: Jellies, seaweed, mussels, and more were washed over the sand berm and headed to the lagoon. The day was immensely foggy. The remainder of the photos were taken at low tide around 5:30 PM, when many more were washing in.
